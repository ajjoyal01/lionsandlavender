ABOUT

This six-piece pop rock band from Boston, MA delivers high-energy performances and infectious tunes that stick with you long after the show is over. With a powerhouse female vocalist leading the charge, their music blends catchy hooks and driving beats that get crowds moving. From feel-good anthems to emotional ballads, their sound is all about fun, connection, and creating unforgettable experiences for their fans!
